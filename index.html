01 · THE PROBLEM

Four systems.
Zero connection.

Every tool does its job. But none of them talk to each other. A customer buys a bike, finances it through Synchrony, brings it back for a warranty issue, and emails a question. No one at Iowa Bike Co can see all of that in one place.

01 / In-store
Ascend RMS
Customer records, purchase history, repair work orders, warranty registrations. The most important data — locked in a POS system with no way out.
Trapped in POS
02 / Web orders
Workstand
Online store, product catalog, web purchases. Orders eventually sync back to Ascend — but in a separate timeline your team can't cross-reference in real time.
Separate system
03 / Financing
Synchrony
Trek Card financing for big-ticket bikes. Who's financed, when their promo period ends, what that means for follow-up — completely invisible to the shop.
Black box
04 / Outreach
Email & phone
Every customer follow-up, service notification, and warranty question handled ad hoc. No log, no templates, no record of who said what or when.
Someone's inbox

The result: a customer who bought a Trek two years ago, financed it, brought it in for warranty work, and emailed once — looks like a stranger every time they walk through the door.

What we build
02 · WHAT WE BUILD

One tool. Built
on what you have.

We don't replace Ascend, Workstand, or Synchrony. We sit on top of all of them — pulling every data point about a customer into a single back-office view your team can act on. Five sections, one pipeline.

01 / Customers
Every customer, fully in view
One record per person — every bike they own, every purchase (in-store and web), every service visit, their financing status, and the full communication history. Not scattered. One scroll.
02 / Sales
In-store and web, one timeline
Ascend in-store sales and Workstand web orders combined. See what your customers are buying, when, and how much — without switching between two systems and manually joining the dots.
03 / Warranty
Know before they call
Warranty status per bike, open claims, and automatic expiry flags. When a customer calls about their Trek warranty, you already have everything — serial number, purchase date, claim history.
04 / Pipeline
The follow-up list that runs itself
Tune-ups overdue, Synchrony promo periods ending, high-value customers who haven't been in — the pipeline surfaces them automatically, with one-click outreach ready to go. Nothing slips.
05 / Communication
Reach out from the record
Email and SMS templates for every scenario — service ready, tune-up due, financing ending soon. Send directly from the customer record, log it automatically, and always know what was sent.
Built on your existing stack
No replacements. No migrations. No downtime.
